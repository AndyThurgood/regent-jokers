Legislation

The problem with an under regulated industry...

Tenants trying to escalate issues or seek help through legal channels have little supports in the form of governing bodies or legislation. The above regulators do not seem to have the power to really make any impactful change.


Right to manage

Tenants have the option to choose who is responsible for the management of their property. Unfortunately the process of exercising the right to management a property is currently slow, expensive and has a number of conditions that favour the management companies.

This should not stop tenants and leaseholders from looking into the RTM path!
